Development Process

1

Requirements Gathering and Analysis

As a developer, my first task is to understand the goals and objectives of the project, the target audience, the platform to focus on, and the process flow and behavior of the feature. I will collaborate with stakeholders to gather requirements and analyze them thoroughly to ensure that the final product meets their expectations.

2

Design

Once I have a clear understanding of the requirements, I will proceed to create a wireframe, high-fidelity mockup, or prototype depending on the project's needs. My objective is to review the output and ensure its feasibility, researching and implementing the best strategies to achieve the requirements. I will collaborate with a designer on the team if necessary.

3

Development

Once the design is approved, I will begin transforming it into a fully functional application while prioritizing clean, readable, and maintainable code. In parallel, I will develop the necessary APIs while considering the responsiveness of the application and ensuring seamless integration with the API.

4

QA and Testing

Once the development is complete, I will review the output of my work and ensure that all use cases have been considered. I will test the application thoroughly to identify and fix any bugs or issues before deployment.

5

Deployment & Maintenance

After UAT testing, I will assist in the deployment, continue testing, or depending on the process. I will monitor updates and performance, ensuring that any unconsidered scenarios and bugs are addressed promptly. Ongoing maintenance is crucial to ensure the application remains reliable and up-to-date.
